About Me

My name is Kaleb Carter and I am a web developer that also has an interest in data science. This past year, I graduated from the University of Oklahoma with a degree in Accounting and Management Information Systems, but recently I have developed a growing interest for software engineering in these past few months. So, I decided to use this time of economic hardship to learn a new skill and excel at the task, and I have loved every second of the journey.


I made this site to showcase some of my projects that I have been working on while I learn. Currently, I am working towards both proficiency and to excel in the following languages: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python. My goal is to work as a full-stack web developer and become an expert in the craft. As I believe my contribution I could be able to make at an organization would be significant even with my lack of experience due to my ability to learn and adapt.


If you wish to contact me for any reason at all, whether you're an employer, fellow coder looking to collaborate on a project, or just want to talk, shoot me a text, email, or give me a call! I am always looking to help and work on projects to improve myself!
